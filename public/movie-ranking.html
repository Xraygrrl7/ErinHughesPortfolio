Erin's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Twister

I love watching storms and this is the ultimate storm movie. There is nothing like watching storm chasers in action.

Some Like It Hot

Marilyn Monroe movies are always great, but this movie with Tony Curtis and Jack Lemmon dressed as women is super funny.

The Wizard of Oz

There's no place like home...who doesn't love this classic fantasy movie. I used to watch this at night when I couldn't sleep.
